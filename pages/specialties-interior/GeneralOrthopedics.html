General orthopedics

Care and treatment to relieve pain and improve your quality of life

Together the bones, joints, tendons, ligaments, nerves and muscles make up the musculoskeletal system. Our orthopedic physicians focus on the diagnosis, prevention, correction, and treatment of conditions and disorders for these parts of body.

General orthopedics is the basis of all specialized and advanced orthopedic study. Our highly trained doctors and surgeons stay up to date on the latest research thanks in part to our connection to the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Our extensive and evidence-based practice focuses on the full spectrum of orthopedic care. Our doctors have the tools and training to deliver care that is personalized to each patient no matter what concerns they have.

Conditions

General orthopedic conditions we treat include:

  • Arthritis of the upper and lower extremities
  • Bursitis
  • Cartilage injuries of the upper and lower extremities
  • Fractures of the upper and lower extremities
  • Muscle and ligament sprains, strains and tears of the upper and lower extremities
  • Osteoarthritis of the upper and lower extremities
  • Traumatic arthritis

Treatment

  • Arthroscopic surgery of the upper and lower extremities
  • Cartilage surgery of the upper and lower extremities
  • Fracture treatment of the upper and lower extremities
  • Imaging
  • Injections
  • Nerve decompression surgery of the upper and lower extremities
  • Tendon repair of the upper and lower extremities
  • Trigger point injections
